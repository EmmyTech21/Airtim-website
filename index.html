Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your questions about our airtime and data services.

How do I purchase airtime and data?

To purchase airtime or data, simply click the "Buy Airtime" or "Buy Data" button on the homepage. You'll be taken to a secure checkout page where you can enter your payment details and complete the transaction.

How do I manage my account?

Manage your account by logging in through the "Login" button at the top of the homepage. From there, you can view your purchase history, update your profile, and adjust your service settings.

What are the benefits of registering?

Registering with us allows you to quickly purchase airtime and data, track your usage, and receive updates on special offers and discounts directly to your email.

How do I check my balance?

You can check your balance by accessing the "Account" section after logging in. Your balance and other relevant details will be displayed on your dashboard.
